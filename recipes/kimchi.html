Kimchi Recipe

What is Kimchi?

Kimchi is a traditional Korean dish made from fermented vegetables, most commonly napa cabbage and Korean radishes, seasoned with variety of spices. Kimchi is often served as a side dish in Korean cuisine but can also be used as a main ingredient in soups stews, and fried rice. It's well known for its probiotic benefits, as the fermentation process promotes healthy gut bacteria, making it not flavorful but also nutritious.
